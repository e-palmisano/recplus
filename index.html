Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my recordings saved?

Recordings are saved as .m4a files in Music › AudioRecorder Sessions inside your home folder. Transcripts are saved next to each recording as .txt files.

Why does Rec+ ask for microphone access?

Rec+ needs microphone permission to record audio. You can grant or revoke it anytime in System Settings › Privacy & Security › Microphone.

Is my audio sent anywhere?

No. Recording and transcription happen entirely on your Mac. Rec+ makes no network calls with your audio or transcripts. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I rename or delete a recording?

Right-click a recording in the sidebar and choose Rename or Delete. Renaming keeps the audio file and its transcript together.

Which macOS versions are supported?

Rec+ requires macOS 26 or later, since transcription uses Apple's on-device SpeechAnalyzer framework.
