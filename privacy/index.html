Rec+ Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Rec+ ("the App") records system audio and microphone input on your Mac and transcribes speech on-device. This policy explains what data the App handles.

Data Collection

Rec+ does not collect, transmit, or share any data. All audio recordings and transcripts stay on your device — nothing is uploaded to servers, third parties, or the developer.

Permissions Used

PermissionPurpose
MicrophoneRecord your voice during a session.
System audio captureRecord the Mac's audio output, using Apple's public CoreAudio process-tap API.
Speech RecognitionLive transcription, fully on-device via Apple's SpeechAnalyzer — no audio or text is sent to Apple or anyone else for this purpose.
User-selected file accessSave recordings and transcripts only to locations you pick.

Third Parties

Rec+ includes no analytics, advertising, trackers, or third-party SDKs. No account, sign-in, or network connection is required to use the App.

Data Storage & Deletion

Recordings and transcripts are saved as local files in the location you choose. Deleting them removes them permanently — Rec+ keeps no copy anywhere else.

Children's Privacy

Rec+ does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
